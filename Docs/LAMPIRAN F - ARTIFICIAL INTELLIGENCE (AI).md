ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE (AI)
IMPACT ASSESSMENT CHECKLIST
Objective: Step-by-step checklist that an AI project team must complete before a new AI system can
be approved. The checklist ensures that every aspect of the AI Impact Assessment (AIIA)
is thoroughly considered.
Project Name: Date:
Project Owner:
STEP 1 SYSTEM AND DATA OVERVIEW
□ System Purpose: Clearly define the AI system's goal and its intended use.
□ AI Technology: Identify the type of AI technology (e.g., machine learning, deep learning,
natural language processing).
□ Data: Specify the data used for training and operation, including its sources, volume, and
type (e.g., personal data, sensitive data).
□ Actors & Stakeholders: List all parties involved in the system's lifecycle (developers,
operators, end-users, affected individuals).
□ Decision-Making: Describe the level of autonomy of the AI system and the nature of the
decisions it makes.
STEP 2 RISK AND IMPACT IDENTIFICATION
Ethical Risk:
□ Bias & Discrimination: Does the system produce biased outcomes based on race,
gender, age, or other protected characteristics?
□ Fairness: Is the system's impact fair and equitable across all stakeholder groups?
□ Human Autonomy & Control: Is there a clear human-in-the-loop for critical decisions?
□ Transparency & Explainability: Can the system's decisions and recommendations be
understood and explained to affected individuals?
Legal & Regulatory Risks:
□ Data Protection: Does the system comply with data protection regulations? Is a Data
Protection Impact Assessment required?
□ Legal Consequences: Are there any legal or financial liabilities associated with the
system's decisions?
□ Industry-Specific Regulations: Does the system comply with AIGE and shariah
standard requirement?
Societal & Human Risks:
□ Social Impact: What is the potential impact on employment, social cohesion, and well-
being?
□ Public Trust: What is the potential impact on public trust in the organization and the
technology?
□ Physical & Psychological Harm: Could the system cause physical or psychological
harm to individuals?
STEP 3 MITIGATION & GOVERNANCE
□ Risk Mitigation Plan: Outline specific measures to mitigate identified risks (e.g., using
debiased data, implementing human oversight).
Technical Safeguards:
□ Robustness & Reliability: What measures are in place to ensure the system is reliable
and performs as expected?
□ Security: Have security vulnerabilities been addressed?
Accountability & Oversight:
□ Designated Owner: Who is responsible for the AI system and its impacts?
□ Auditing: Is there a plan for a regular internal or external audit of the system?

AIIA Checklist
□ Recourse Mechanisms: Is there a process for individuals to appeal a decision made by
the AI system?
□ Documentation: Is the entire assessment process, including decisions and rationale, fully
documented?
STEP 4 FINAL ASSESSMENT & APPROVAL
□ Overall Risk Rating: Assign an overall risk rating to the AI project (e.g., Low, Medium,
High).
□ Recommendations: Provide a clear recommendation on whether to proceed with the
project, including any required conditions or modifications.
□ Stakeholder Sign-off: Obtain sign-off from relevant stakeholders (e.g., legal, compliance,
ethics committee, senior leadership).
Note: This checklist is intended to provide a structured and thorough approach to assessing an AI system's
potential impact, serving as a guide rather than an exhaustive list.
